Chilli Recipe

Easy to make chili recipe!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Beans
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Chili Powder, Cumin, Organo, Garlic Powder, Salt, and Pepper

Steps

  1. Saute the vegtables
  2. Brown the meat
  3. Add the seasoning
  4. Add the water
  5. Let it simmer
  6. Enjoy!
